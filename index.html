About Us

We supply products with a focus on recycled resources

We supply products that benefit both people and the environment. We provide unrivalled local capabilities, long-term leadership, and deep innovation expertise to customers of all sizes all over the world.

Our mission is to collaborate with local communities, organizations, and manufacturers to create a network that maximizes the collection and utilization of textile waste, fostering a culture of sustainability.

We envision a world where textile waste is regarded as a valuable resource, and our company plays a pivotal role in unlocking its full potential through innovative and scalable solutions.
